Student ideas

Fast topics for a multi-page personal site

Each topic below is easy to finish in one lesson and can be expanded later with CSS and JavaScript.

My Hobby Club

Tell about your hobby, equipment, top 5 tips, and useful links.

Great for: sports, music, drawing

Game Guide

Build a mini-guide with game overview, character list, and beginner strategy.

Great for: gamers and stream fans

City Explorer

Create a page about your town with places to visit and photo points.

Great for: local culture projects

Future Profession

Describe your dream profession, required skills, and a personal learning plan.

Great for: career orientation

Recommended page structure

  1. Home: title, short intro, navigation menu.
  2. About: details, list of interests, one image.
  3. Projects: 2-3 cards with descriptions.
  4. Contact: safe public links and publish checklist.
